About Us

About Us TechNova is a global leader in print solutions, offering innovative and world-class products across a wide range of industries – including Commercial & Newspaper Printing, Publishing, Packaging, Signage, Photo, Textile, Engineering. Recognised worldwide as a pioneer, TechNova has introduced breakthrough technologies and transformative products that have redefined print processes and created new markets. With a robust distribution and logistics network, TechNova's products and services are trusted by virtually every printer in India and reach customers across more than 60 countries through its international offices and partnerships. At the heart of TechNova’s growth is a strong value system – a strict code of ethics, a commitment to excellence, business conduct based on honesty, fairness, mutual trust and an unwavering "Customer First" philosophy.
